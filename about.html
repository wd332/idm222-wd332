Hello! I'm Wendy.

I am a User Experience & Interaction Design (UXID) student from Drexel University.

I first discovered my love for design through a project called The Phoenix Lantern, where I explored how the fusion of physical form and symbolism could evoke emotion and meaning. Since then, I've worked on digital and interactive projects that helped me understand how people experience design — not just through visuals or usability, but through feeling and storytelling.

I'm currently exploring how design can bridge the digital and physical — creating experiences that invite curiosity, emotion, and connection.

Outside of design, I enjoy reading, experiencing new things, and traveling.

I'd love to get to know more about people, about their story and experiences, so let me know via email or on LinkedIn if you want to chat!
