The Phoenix Lantern

  • PRODUCT DESIGN
  • PRODUCT RESEARCH
  • IDEATION
  • STORYTELLING
  • PROTOTYPING

Through combining the qualities of the mythical creature Phoenix and a lantern through research, analysis, ideation, and designing, a new product was born! A modern lantern taking on the features of the Phoenix helping to connect people all around the world through its creative word design and the assistance of an app.

Research

Through researching the historical and cultural importance of the Phoenix and a lantern using online resources and user research, I was able to empathize with them and understand how they see things and how others perceive them.

Ideation

Through mindmapping, I was able to find a similarity between them that they both have "connection". The Phoenix has stories told all over the world, across different religions and myths. Lanterns are used by people in the past to see through the dark, and have advanced through knowledge passed down through generations to become the light bulbs we have today.

"How might we create a lantern that somehow connects people together like the Phoenix did?"

Design Solution

The Phoenix lantern creates an opportunity for people to connect with others all around the world through the touch of their phone and the presence of their lantern.

It is multifunctional with key features being that it can:

  • fly (covers more areas for light to reach and represents like the Phoenix flying)
  • receive/send inspirational quotes to light someone's day or for general conversations (connecting with others far away)
  • change colors depending on how far another person with this lantern is (Phoenix is known to have different colors).
